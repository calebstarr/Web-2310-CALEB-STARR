Our Philosophy

Cologne for good

At EVERSCENT, we believe a picture is worth a thousand words. What the bottle looks like has nothing to do with the contents. That's why you won't find a misleadingly ornate cologne bottle image anywhere on our site. What we show you is the scent visualized: A picture of the namesake of the scent and a personification of what that cologne embodies. Feel free to explore until you discover the scent that speaks to you. Here, it's only a matter of time before you find the thing you've been missing all along.

Here at EVERSCENT, you can also rest assured knowing that money spent on our radically inventive scents is going toward a good cause: sharing the gift of food and housing with the less fortunate of the community. So go on, splurge and spoil yourself. It's for the greater good, after all. We don't let any of our profits go to waste. If it isn't put into making more wonderful rich fragrances, it foes back into the people of this community. EVERSCENT will always be about doing good for the consumer and the underprivileged.
